Capabilities

Four disciplines, stacked into one workflow.

Each capability below is handled in-house and reviewed against the one before it, the same way a board's copper, silkscreen and soldermask layers have to agree before anything gets etched.

L1 — SCHEMATIC

Circuit design

Component selection, schematic capture and simulation that catches costly mistakes before a single trace gets drawn.

L2 — LAYOUT

Layout & signal integrity

Trace routing, stackup planning and impedance control tuned for the frequencies your board actually needs to carry.

L3 — FABRICATION

Fabrication sourcing

Vetted fab partners for rigid, flex and rigid-flex boards, matched to your tolerance, material and volume needs.

L4 — ASSEMBLY

Assembly & test

SMT and through-hole population, AOI inspection and functional test before a board ever leaves the line.

How a project moves

From a rough idea to a tested board.

Five stages, run in order, each with its own review before the project is allowed to move forward.

01

Consult

We scope requirements, constraints and target cost together with your team.

02

Schematic

Circuit design and component sourcing, checked against availability and cost.

03

Layout

Routing, stackup and DFM review so the design is manufacturable on the first pass.

04

Prototype

A small batch is fabricated and assembled for bring-up and functional testing.

05

Production

Approved designs move to volume fabrication, assembly and shipping.
